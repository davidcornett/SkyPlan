Sky Plan Tool

Find out the best time of year, and sometimes even the best year, to observe astronomical objects. Whether you are into astrophotography, planning a star party, or just looking a great view of your favorite planet or Deep Sky Object, timing is everything:

  • Planetary close approaches
  • DSO and planetary position in the sky
  • Expected seeing conditions based on historical averages
  • Light pollution in your location
  • Lunar brightness
